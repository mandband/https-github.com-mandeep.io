How to take a mindful minute.

  • Sit at your desk, clear it from worksheets, highlighters and green pens.
  • Take a drink from that flask you fill in the morning but never finish.
  • Place your hands in a balanced position and close your eyes. Block out the garish displays posted by the person who had your room seven years ago.
  • Ignore the sounds of screaming from the playground outside.
  • Focus on your breathing, as you follow each breath in and out.
  • After 1 minute, gradually open your eyes and resume activities.
  • Do you need the bathroom? Go. It's not worth the UTI.
